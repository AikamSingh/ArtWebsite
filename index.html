About Me

`

Hi, I’m Aikam Singh, an artist with a deep passion for exploring the emotional and cultural connections that art creates. My journey started as a child, painting alongside my Nana, who taught me the beauty of capturing not just what we see, but what we feel. Those early mornings spent sketching and painting instilled in me a lifelong love for artistic expression.

I draw inspiration from nature, travel, the games I play, and the diverse cultures I've experienced, seeking to tell stories through my work that resonate with people on a personal level. Whether it's through intricate sketches or bold, expressive pieces, I aim to convey the emotions and moments that shape our world.

Art is my way of communicating and connecting with others, and I’m always excited to discover new techniques, mediums, and inspirations.

Aikam Singh 2024
